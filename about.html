About Our Practice

We are a collaborative team of a Registered Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioner (R.TCMP) and Registered Massage Therapist (RMT) committed to patient-centered, evidence-informed care. Our clinic blends classical TCM techniques with modern wellness strategies to help patients achieve balance and long-term health.

Leona Zhao

Leona is a Registered Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioner (R.TCMP) with a strong passion for holistic healing and natural therapies. She provides comprehensive treatments that integrate advanced needling techniques, Chinese herbal medicine, fire cupping, Guasha, and more. Leona addresses a wide range of conditions with a balanced and patient-focused approach, offering individualized care rooted in both classical practice and modern evidence. Her deep commitment to supporting the body's natural healing potential shines through every treatment session.

Lindon Wu (Lin Dong)

Lindon is a dedicated Registered Massage Therapist with a focus on holistic treatment and body alignment. A graduate of Georgian College in Ontario, Lindon is known for incorporating a wide variety of therapeutic techniques including Swedish massage, deep tissue work, joint mobilization, fascial techniques, and therapeutic stretches. He creates personalized treatment plans that prioritize mobility, balance, and long-term wellness. With a commitment to continued learning and evidence-informed care, Lindon ensures each client receives compassionate and effective therapy tailored to their needs.
