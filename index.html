At Treat Together, healing is guided by phronēsis — the ancient Greek concept of practical wisdom. This principle shapes how we work: with care, presence, and insight, drawing on experience and intuition to treat each person as whole and unique. It's not just what we do — it's how we do it.

Traditional Chinese Medicine & Acupuncture

Rooted in centuries of Eastern wisdom, Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) sees health as a dynamic balance of energies within the body. Through acupuncture, Chinese herbal medicine, cupping, and Guasha, we support your body's innate ability to heal and restore harmony. Leona Zhao (R.TCMP, R.Ac) brings a deep respect for classical techniques alongside modern, personalized care.

Massage Therapy & Western Clinical Care

Massage therapy is a cornerstone of modern physical wellness — grounded in anatomy, science, and therapeutic technique. With an intuitive and evidence-informed approach, Lindon Wu (RMT) combines Swedish massage, deep tissue work, and joint mobilization to restore movement, reduce pain, and support long-term function and balance.

Our approach is integrative, collaborative, and intentional. We bring East and West together through hands-on care, clear communication, and a commitment to ethical, patient-centered healing — all grounded in the practice of phronēsis.
